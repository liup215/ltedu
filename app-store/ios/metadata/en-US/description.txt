LTEdu is a comprehensive smart education platform designed to help students achieve their academic goals through personalized learning plans, real-time progress tracking, and data-driven insights.

KEY FEATURES

Personalized Learning Plans
AI-generated long-term, mid-term, and short-term study plans tailored to each student's target exam and current level.

Progress Tracking
Visualize learning progress with detailed analytics, heatmaps, and performance trends.

Question Bank
Access thousands of curated questions organized by syllabus, knowledge node, and difficulty level.

Practice & Assessment
Complete practice sets and mock exams with automatic grading and answer explanations.

Early Warning System
Receive intelligent alerts when learning goals are at risk, enabling timely intervention.

Teacher & Class Management
Educators can manage classes, assign plans, and monitor all students from a single dashboard.

Multi-Role Support
Supports students, teachers, and administrators with role-based access and tailored experiences.

LTEdu bridges the gap between education goals and daily study habits, giving every student a clear, structured path to success.
